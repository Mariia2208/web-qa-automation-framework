## TC-001: Sort products by Price (Low to High)

**Priority:** Medium  
**Type:** Functional  

### Preconditions:
- User is on the products listing page
- There are multiple products with different prices

### Steps:
1. Open the products page
2. Select "Price: Low to High" from the sort dropdown

### Expected Result:
- Products are displayed from the lowest price to the highest price
- No products are missing
- Sorting is consistent after page reload
